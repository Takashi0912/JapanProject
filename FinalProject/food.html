FOOD GUIDE JAPAN

The 10 Most Breathtaking Secret Food in Japan

A list of food that our editors believe you should eat

"Food is a journey, it delights the senses. All the rest is mere hunger and longing."

1. Ryokan Dinner

Experience traditional Japanese hospitality with a Ryokan (Japanese style hotel) dinner. Enjoy a multi-course feast featuring seasonal ingredients, beautifully presented in a serene, authentic setting.

2. Sushi

Indulge in the true iconic flavors of Japan with fresh, hand-pressed sushi. Savor the delicate balance of vinegared rice and the finest cuts of raw fish, a must-try culinary delight.

3. Sukiyaki

Warm up with a pot of Sukiyaki, a flavorful hot pot dish. Thinly sliced beef, tofu, and vegetables simmered in a sweet soy-based sauce, perfect for a cozy meal.

4. KatsuDon

Enjoy the comforting taste of Katsudon, a bowl of rice topped with a crispy, breaded pork cutlet, eggs, and savory sauce. It's a delicious and satisfying meal.

5. Kaisen Don

Dive into a bowl of Kaisen Don, a seafood rice bowl topped with an array of fresh sashimi. It's a vibrant and refreshing way to taste the ocean's bounty.

6. Okonomiyaki

Relish the fun and flavors of Okonomiyaki, a savory pancake filled with cabbage, meat, or seafood. Topped with a rich sauce, mayo, and bonito flakes, it's a taste sensation.

7. Unagi Kabayaki

Savor the rich and smoky taste of Unagi Kabayaki, grilled eel glazed with a sweet soy sauce. Served over rice, it's a delicacy you won't forget.

8. Takoyaki

Treat yourself to Takoyaki, delicious octopus balls with a crispy exterior and creamy center. Topped with savory sauce, mayo, and bonito flakes, they are a popular street food.

9. Wahuu Bento

Enjoy a taste of Japan on the go with a Wahuu Bento, a traditional Japanese lunch box. Packed with a variety of dishes like rice, fish, pickles, and more, it's a complete meal in a box.

10. Wagashi

Delight in the elegance of Wagashi, traditional Japanese sweets. Made from ingredients like sweet red bean paste, rice flour, and seasonal fruits, these confections are as beautiful as they are delicious.
